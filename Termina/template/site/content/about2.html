Solving the cost problem

There's no need to repeat other career's path

With the blooming of DeFi in 2020 so far, DeFi's capitalization has reached more than 50 billion USD by July 2021. A rapid growth, leading to the cost of joining the network becoming more and more expensive and not suitable for microtransactions. Part of that is because the cost with DeFi operations has not really been cut to the maximum. DeFi boasts low costs but due to the strong price volatility of collateralized coins. And if the loan contract is liquidated, users will have to pay a stable fee ranging from 11 to 13%.
